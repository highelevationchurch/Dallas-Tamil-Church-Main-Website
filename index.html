WELCOME

We invite you to the Dallas Tamil Church located in Carrollton, TX. The church has a unique vision of sharing the gospel of Jesus Christ to the Tamil speaking community in the Dallas Metroplex. We share the gospel of Jesus Christ to the people of South Indian Origin who seek to worship the Lord in their own mother tongue (Tamil). As we praise and worship God in this beautiful, rich language, we hope that our friends will know the Lord Jesus and experience the love and power of the Holy Spirit in our church services. We also have English services for the Youth and several other ministries that you can participate in. May the Lord bless you as you join in this wonderful orchestrated effort to build the Kingdom of God on earth.

- Pr. Dr. John Henry Mathias and Sis.Christina Henry
