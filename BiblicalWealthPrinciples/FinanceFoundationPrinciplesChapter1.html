Finance Foundation Principles
 
Much of our life is impacted by Finances. Solomon said, "Money answers everything". Money answers every acquisition of every material thing. We need to understand the biblical view of Finances to handle finances effectively, to come out of debt, to leave inheritance to our children. In this lesson, we shall study the fundamentals of Finance.
 
1. God made Gold

Conclusion
God is the author and finisher of our faith. God is also the author and owner of every wealth and resources. When we seek Him and keep His commandments, we will become good stewards of His wealth. May the Lord make us his faithful stewards through Christ Jesus. Amen.
