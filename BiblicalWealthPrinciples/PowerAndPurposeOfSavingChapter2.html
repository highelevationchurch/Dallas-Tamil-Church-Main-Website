Biblical Wealth Principles
 
Chapter - 2
 
 
Power & Purpose of Saving
 
Introduction
The contemporary culture focuses on spending before we even earn. There is strong pull to acquire things that we do not need, and to buy things before we actually need them. By buying continuously, there is no drive to save the wealth that Gives. In this lesson, we will look at the power of Saving, and purpose of Saving.

Conclusion
Today is the time to save! As time rolls by, everything we save, will become a legacy to our children.Properties are also a resource for the Children, to generate wealth in times of famine.

It is a privilege to handle wealth. We are encouraged to work hard and to multiply our talents, without burying our talents. We are asked to leave legacy to our children just like Abraham who gave all the wealth to Isaac. We are asked to give for the gospel work such as Church Planting,, Orphanages, Digging bore wells, buying bicycles for Pastors, and supporting missionary work.

May the Lord enlarge our territory and fill our barns so that we can be faithful stewards for the expansion of His Kingdom.
