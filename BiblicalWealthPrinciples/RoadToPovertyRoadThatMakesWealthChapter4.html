Conclusion
The road to poverty is decorated with Ungodliness, Laziness, and Ignorance. The road to wealth is decorated with lips of Knowledge, Godly wisdom, and Disciplined hands that sow and reap. Wealth cannot be earned quickly, but it can surely be earned little by little with the application of knowledge and skills that the Lord provides, so that we all can have all sufficiency in all things in our life.
