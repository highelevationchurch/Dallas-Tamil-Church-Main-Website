Chapter - 10
 
 
Honesty in Finance
 
Introduction

From the boardroom to the petty shops, there is a cry for honesty in financial matters. God wants us to be honest in our financial matters. If we are faithful in little, God will set us over much. In this lesson, we will study the role of Honesty in building Financial Wealth.

4. Teach Honesty to your Children
  1. Honest has to be taught to our children. When a child brings a pen or pencil or dollar, from the school that is not his, we must rebuke that behavior and ask the Child to return it to the School. The Child should be taught the virtue of Honesty in small things such as the stolen pencil.

5. Dishonesty dwindles wealth
  1. A stolen property will eventually leave us more broke. If the clerk in the shop charges for only 3 items, when we have bought 4 items, we should immediately bring that to the clerk's attention.

6. Bribes blind a person
  1. A bribe is anything given to a person to influence him to do something illegal or wrong (Crown, 2003). Bribes are prohibited in scripture. We must refrain from receiving bribes and giving bribes. We must totally depend on the Lord for financial victory.

Conclusion

God demands absolute honesty. The wealth of the righteous will increase but the wealth gained through dishonesty will dwindle. May the Holy Spirit direct our words, thoughts and actions, as we build our financial future truly upon the word of God. God will surely bless the righteous, as He blessed Abraham.
