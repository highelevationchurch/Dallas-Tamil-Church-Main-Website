Chapter - 8
 
 
Art of Budgeting to Win
 
Introduction

Most of the time, it is not the lack of money but the proper use of money that is essential to live a successful, worry-free life financially. Budget is the plan for spending money. It is also defined as the estimate of income and expenditure for a set period of time. Webster dictionary defined budget as, "an amount of money available for spending that is based on a plan for how it will be spent".

In this lesson, we will study on the art of Budgeting.

  1. Being content is the biblical way to be happy in all circumstances. Accumulation of wealth does not bring happiness. Rather, being content in every circumstance is the key to happiness.

      I can do all things through Christ which strengtheneth me.
  2. The ideas given below can help us to come out of debt and to budget expenditures. You can choose the ones that are applicable to your situation and it will vary from person to person.
    1. Shelter
      1. Plan a modest home suitable to your needs and also expandable to meet your future needs
      2. Apartment living can be less expensive with less lawn care, maintenance for sometime
      3. Taking care of lawn, carpet cleaning, painting and pest control can bring substantial amount
      4. Limit the use of lights, heaters and appliances
    2. Food
      1. Prepare a menu for week and shop according to the list
      2. Shop once a week
      3. Reduce ready-made food which has labor added to its price
      4. Leave children at home while shopping
      5. Reduce use of paper products (plates, cups) which are expensive
    3. Transportation
      1. Use one car to travel if it is possible
      2. Purchase a low-cost used car until repairs become too expensive
      3. Smallest car is most economical to use.
      4. Routine maintenance will increase longevity of vehicles
    4. Clothing
      1. Make a list of clothes you need and shop from that list
      2. Purchase home-washable fabrics. Commercial cleaning are expensive
    5. Insurance
      1. Select insurance based on need and budget
      2. Raising deductible feature will reduce premiums substantially
      3. A capable agent can save money
    6. Health
      1. Proper sleep, exercise and nutrition is required to stay health
      2. Prevention is better than cure
      3. Practice proper oral hygiene for healthy teeth and to reduce dental bills
      4. Visit competent physicians and dentists when needed
    7. Entertainment and Recreation
      1. Plan and save for vacation
      2. Plan your vacation in off-season and closer to home
      3. Expensive entertainment can be replaced with creative alternatives such as picnics and free state parks
    8. Hings
      1. Each month, reconcile the check books
      2. Think yearly. Figure out not only monthly and but also yearly costs.
        1. $6 for lunch each day times 5 days a week, times 50 weeks a year gives $1500. Forgoing lunch can save significant amounts in a year. Do the same for other routine costs.
      3. Avoid instinct spending
        1. Do not purchase car in 1 day. Pray about it for few weeks
        2. As you pray, the instinct will soon pass away
      4. It is wise for husbands and wives to include personal allowances in budget, to spend as they please. This will eliminate arguments.
        1. Husband can play golf within the budget
        2. Wife can participate in her favorite hobby.

  1. Do not stop to budget. A budget properly used can save 1000s of dollars. Write it each month and revise it. But do not stop budgeting.
