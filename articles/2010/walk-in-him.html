Walking with Jesus

Col 2:6 says, �As you have therefore received Christ Jesus the Lord, so walk in Him�

1. Walk in God�s Word

  • Our walk must be saturated with the Word of God. Hosea says that God�s people are destroyed for lack of knowledge (Hosea 4:6). Hosea further writes, �Let us pursue the knowledge of the LORD since His going forth is established as the morning; He will come to us like the rain, like the latter and former rain to the earth� (Hosea 6:3).
  • Walking with God also ensures success in our lives. Blessed is the man whose delight is in the law of the LORD and in His law he meditates day and night. He shall be like a tree planted by the rivers of water, that brings forth its fruit in its season, Whose leaf shall not wither and whatever he does shall prosper (Psalm 1:2-3).
  • Joshua was commanded to walk in the law of the Lord to ensure success (Joshua 1:8)

2. Walk in Talking

  • We must guard our thoughts and words. Walking with Christ enables us to speak the right words. Our words are sanctified when we walk in Him. Paul, while writing to Colossians says that we must walk in wisdom to those who are outside, redeeming the time (Col 4:5).
  • Our speech should always be seasoned with salt, that we might know how we ought to answer each one (Col 4:6)

3. Walk in Conduct

  • Our conduct should be as that of in Christ. We must be not only hearers of the Word, but also doers of the Word.
  • Peter commends us to walk in Christ in good conduct. �Having a good conscience, that when they defame you as evildoers, those who revile your good conduct in Christ may be ashamed� (1 Peter 3:16)

Yours in Christ
John Henry Mathias
